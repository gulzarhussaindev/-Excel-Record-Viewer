About Excel Record Viewer

Excel Record Viewer is a secure, fast, and user-friendly tool designed to let you open Excel and CSV files directly in your browser — with no installation and zero privacy risk.

Why This Tool Was Created

Many “online viewers” secretly upload files to remote servers. Excel Record Viewer does the opposite — it processes everything 100% offline inside your browser, powered by secure JavaScript technology.

  • Client-side processing only — Your data never leaves your device.
  • No servers or databases — Nothing is uploaded or stored.
  • Fast & lightweight — Works instantly in any modern browser.
  • PWA support — Install on desktop or mobile for offline use.
