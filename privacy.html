Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 21, 2025

Your Privacy Matters

Excel Record Viewer is a 100% client-side tool. Your Excel or CSV files never leave your device — they are processed locally within your browser.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal or file data.

How Your Data Is Processed

Your files are parsed inside your browser memory using JavaScript. No file content is stored, logged, uploaded, or transmitted.

Third-Party Services

We use Google Analytics to understand visits (e.g., number of users), but your file contents are never included.
