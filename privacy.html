Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 27, 2025

Overview

Excel Record Viewer is a client-side web application. All file parsing and editing happen locally inside your browser. We do not upload, store, or process your file contents on our servers.

Files & data

Your uploaded Excel or CSV file is read into your browser memory for viewing and editing. We do not retain copies of your file on any remote server.

Edits & Exports

Any edits or new records are kept in the browser session. To persist changes outside your device you must use the Export feature — that creates a downloadable file (ExcelViewer_Export_YYYY-MM-DD.xlsx) and is delivered to your browser's downloads folder.

Third-party services

We use Google Analytics for anonymous visit metrics. We do not send file contents, only anonymized usage data. You can opt out by blocking analytics scripts or using privacy extensions.
